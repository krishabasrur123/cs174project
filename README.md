Eco-City Simulator is an exciting 3D adventure where you become the hero of a city in need! Explore a vibrant virtual world as you pick up litter, recycle waste, and plant trees to bring nature back to life. As the city becomes cleaner, it transforms before your eyes with lush greenery, returning wildlife, and bright, lively streets. Earn points by using tools and knowledge about environmentally friendly practices like recycling bins and tree planters to boost your efforts. Your ultimate goal is to turn a polluted city into a thriving green paradise, and gain maximum points.
